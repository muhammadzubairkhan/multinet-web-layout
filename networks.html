Long Haul Network

Terrestial Connectivity

Multinet is connected to Afghanistan by virtue of our infrastructure routed through entrances in the northern and southern parts of the neighboring country. Other network expansions aimed at terrestrial connectivity with Iran, China and Central Asian states are underway, aimed at establishing Multinet as the leading gateway to the region. Multinet is available at the border with India ready for cross border network interconnect.

International POPs

Currently, Multinet operates three international POPs in Karachi, London and Singapore enabling domestic customers to reach out global destinations and assist international operators in connecting sites within Pakistan. Multinet is gearing up to launch three new international POPs in Los Angeles, Dubai and Hong Kong within 2016.
